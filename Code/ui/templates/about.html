Chinese Contract Nerd

Our Mission

Chinese Contract Nerd is an advanced, AI-powered legal technology platform designed to assist second parties—such as renters and employees—in identifying unreasonable contracts, ambiguous clauses, and unconscionable terminology specifically within Chinese legal agreements.

Academic Foundation

This application is an extended development based on our cutting-edge research published in the peer-reviewed journal Electronics (2025). The tool represents a real-world implementation of the theories and pipelines we developed for leveraging Large Language Models to shield consumers against unfair contract clauses.
